About Our Company

PT Nusatel Arindo Gutara was established on December 7, 1992 and has more than 30 years of experience in the telecommunications infrastructure industry in Indonesia.

Our company focuses on telecommunications infrastructure development including Civil Works, CME Installation, RF Installation and Optimization, and Transmission Installation. We also provide maintenance services for network systems.

We have successfully worked with major telecommunications operators such as Telkom Indonesia, Indosat Ooredoo, XL Axiata, Smartfren, and Hutchison 3 Indonesia.

🎯 Vision

To become a trusted and leading telecommunications infrastructure company in Indonesia that delivers high quality services and innovative solutions.

🚀 Mission

  • Provide high quality telecommunications services
  • Develop skilled human resources through training
  • Build strong partnerships with customers
  • Support Indonesia's digital infrastructure growth
  • Maintain safety and environmental standards
